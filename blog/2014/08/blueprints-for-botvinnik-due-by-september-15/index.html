Blueprints for Botvinnik Due by September 15

Aug 21, 2014 by johnmark
  • releases
  • collaboration
  • events

The votes are in, and “Botvinnik” is the winner! In planning for Botvinnik (which we’ll sometimes shorten to “bot”) we’re now accepting blueprints for proposed features. Just submit your blueprint and make sure to categorize it properly. Next, you’ll need to propose an abstract for the ManageIQ Design Summit. Make sure to put your proposal in the Abstracts category.

If your blueprints or talks are accepted, you’ll get a free pass to the design summit, as will anyone who has contributed code, documentation or end user support to the community. This is a great opportunity to get involved and influence the direction of the ManageIQ community.

Submit your blueprints by September 15. Abstracts will be selected on a rolling basis until the agenda is full, so the earlier the better!
